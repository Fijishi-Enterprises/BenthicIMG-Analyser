Sources

A Source is CoralNet's name for a coral analysis project.

To get started, create a Source or get invited to a Source. You can choose to make your Source public or private. Once you have a Source, you'll create a labelset, which is the set of labels that you'll use to annotate your images. Image annotation means identifying points of the image: as Acropora coral, Porites coral, algae, and so on.

Once you have a Source and labelset, you can upload images and start annotating them, using our in-browser annotation tool. You can also upload pre-existing annotations. Then, you can view annotation statistics on your entire Source or a subset of images. You can filter or aggregate images by image date and up to 5 custom metadata fields that you define.

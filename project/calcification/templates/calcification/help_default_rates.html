Rates are in units of kg CaCO3 m-2 yr-1. A rate above 0 means that this label's presence tends to contribute to reef carbonate production. A rate below 0 corresponds to carbonate erosion.

Note that only some of CoralNet's labels have rates provided. CoralNet's calcification rates are derived from ReefBudget's data.
